The Three Layers

Most people know their sun sign. It's the one you get when someone asks "what's your sign?" at a party. Understanding all three core placements is the difference between reading a summary of a book and actually reading it.

How to Find Yours

You need three things: your date of birth, your place of birth, and your exact time of birth. The time is the critical one. Without it, your rising sign cannot be calculated accurately.

The Sacred Triad

The Sun: The engine. Your core energy and fundamental drive.

The Moon: The interior. Your emotional world and private experience.

The Rising: The exterior. Your presence and how the world receives you.
